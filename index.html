Biography

Bernadette Koenig was born on 25.03.2003 in Vienna, Austria. Coming from a family of musicians, she soon developed great interest in the Cello, which she then pursued starting from the age of seven. Within the first few years of her musical education, she performed numerous concerts in the Orangerie of SchÃ¶nbrunn Castle, as well as the Vienna City Hall. At the age of eleven, she successfully auditioned for the Young Student Program at the Music and Arts University of the City of Vienna(MUK), where she has been studying under the guidance of Professor Lilia Shulz-Bayrova.

Discography

Since 2015, Bernadette has gained extensive chamber music experience through her work with Evgeny Sinaisky, Christoph Eggner, and Matt Hunt. Alongside her passion for chamber music, she has gained substantial orchestral experience, performing at venues such as the Golden Hall of the Musikverein, the Radiokulturhaus, and the Theater an der Wien. Furthermore, Bernadette won first prize in the Austrian National youth competition "Prima La Musica", in both the chamber music and solo categories n 2015 and 2018.
